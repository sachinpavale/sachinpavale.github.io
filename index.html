My Skill & Experience

I have 2.5 years of experience in Graphic Design, specializing in creating a wide range of marketing materials. My work includes designing website templates, web banners, logos, social media posts, carousels, print materials such as billboards, roll-ups, flyers, posters, food menu cards, invitation cards, ID cards, business cards, labels, stickers, and product packaging etc.

I am proficient in using industry-standard design software like Adobe Creative Suite, which includes Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, as well as CorelDRAW to bring these projects to life.

As a graphic designer, I have encountered various design challenges in my career, often requiring creative solutions and quick turnaround times to meet project goals and deadlines. This is why I try to make each project creative and submit it to the client within the deadline.
